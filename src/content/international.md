We are privileged to work with many churches in support of missionaries and ministries around the world. We provide tangible goods to groups who align with our beliefs. Some items we have shipped internationally are Bibles, hymn books, clothing, blankets, bedding, shoes, food, hygiene items, school supplies, desks, craft supplies, stuffed animals, and toys just to name a few.

<img src="/assets/images/pages/international/map.png" alt="Map of all countries we have shipped supplies to" style="width:100%;max-width:800px;margin-bottom:20px;margin:auto">

***North & Central America:** Antigua & Barbuda, Bahamas, Barbados, Belize, Bermuda, Canada, Costa Rica, Cuba, Dominican Republic, El Salvador, Guatemala, Haiti, Honduras, Jamaica, Mexico, Nicaragua, Panama, Puerto Rico, St. Kitts & Nevis, St. Lucia, St. Vincent & the Grenadines, Trinidad & Tobago, and The United States. **South America:** Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Colombia, Ecuador, Guyana, Peru, Suriname, Uruguay, and Venezuela. **Africa:** Algeria, Angola, Burundi, Democratic Republic, of the Congo, Egypt, Eritea, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Kenya, Liberia, Libya, Madagascar, Morocco, Mozambique, Namibia, Nigeria, Rwanda, Senegal, Sierra leon, Somalia, South Africa, Swaziland, Tanzania, Tunisia, Uganda, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. **Asia:** Armenia, Bahrain, Cambodia, China, Indonesia, India, Iraq, Iran, Israel, Japan, Kuwait, Malaysia, Maldives, Myanmar, Nepal, Oman, Pakistan, Philippines, Qatar, Russia, Singapore, South Korea, Sri Lanka, Taiwan, Thailand, UAE, and Yemen. **Oceania:** Papua New Guinea, and Vanuatu. **Europe:** Belarus, Belgium, Bulgaria, Czechia, Denmark, Italy, Latvia, Netherlands, Spain, Romania, and Ukraine*{#small-text}


Items from our ministry have gone into 105 other countries through non-profits, churches and individuals going on mission trips. School startups in 5 countries have received supplies from us. Hospitals and clinics in 12 countries currently rely on the medical supplies we ship to include a medical college in Congo.

Pictures, updates, and prayer requests come to us regularly from these recipients.
